Rock Paper Scissors

Select one of rock, paper, or scissors and see if you can win against the computer!




Rules to play the game

Probably you have played with your friends during childhood, else, the rules are simple.

  • If you choose Rock, you win against Scissors but lose against Paper.
  • If you choose Scissors, you win against Paper but lose against Rock.
  • If you choose Paper, you win against Rock but lose against Scissors.
